About

My Story

Hi, I'm Gabriella, and I'm a UX designer based in Gaithersburg, Maryland.

I am transitioning to UX after being an elementary school PE teacher. I have always enjoyed problem solving, but my desire for a more artistic career led me to user experience design. My teaching experience has taught me strong communication skills as well as how to balance business requirements with user requirements as I balanced school/student requirements daily. I'm looking forward to using my skills to create the best possible product for all parties.

In my free time I enjoy playing Dungeons and Dragons, running, and making pottery.
